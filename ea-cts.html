Landing Page Design

In this project, I helped to improve the brand image of the CTS team and the services they offer. This involved redesigning and implementing their wiki portal into a landing page that projected professionalism and credibility.

Take a few existing ingredients

 

CTS (Central Technology Services) had a rudimentary landing page within their corporate Wikipedia, but it failed to give them the professional image they were looking for.

They did, however, have a logo and style guide, which they were loosely following.

 

And consider our customers

Our customers were game franchises, such as FIFA, Madden, and NHL, who utilized central team services for common features so they could focus on creating the best games they possible could.

Ultimately, the well-being of CTS depended upon the value they could create for the game teams.

 

Identifying our purpose

We needed to improve the brand image to establish professionalism and credibility in the minds of our customers.

We also needed to communicate the functions of the team and have the page act as a Wikipedia resource to the rest of the organization. Visitors should be directed to where they needed to go quickly and effectively.

Starting the process of iteration:

Layout

Easy access to information: Search, Navigate, Explore.

Identity

A statement of who we are.

Art Direction

An aura of EA games.

Branding

Distinguishing sub-groups through the existing color palette.

Turning it into something that invites the customer

 

Outcome

Because of the abstract logo, it was important to reinforce the image of CTS and its sub-groups through its existing color palette and typography. Overall, the new design better communicated their identity and allowed users to search, navigate, or explore the site more effectively.

My official job title was project analyst where the original plan was to do project management work. After this project, I ended up doing design work for the rest of the work term, helping to brand their team and services through logo design, powerpoint templates, and producing various video content to display through their area.
