PROJECT 2

IAT-235: Information Design

TEAM PROJECT [MY ROLE - DESIGNER AND DEVELOPER]

In this course, we learned about organizing information into a structure and layout that would assist a user through their task. After studying the information and understanding the intended audience, we considered their context of use in order to develop an information architecture, followed by wireframing and iterating through mockups.

The final project was to design a car buying assistant to help a college student select an appropriate vehicle for his or her needs. Rather than check off a list of features, we used a question and answer format in a conversational tone to help derive what vehicle would suit them the best. We implemented a responsive (web and mobile) prototype of the design in HTML5 and CSS.
